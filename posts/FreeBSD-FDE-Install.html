Procedure

Download and prepare the installation media, then boot into it. The next few steps are really straight forward and the handbook does a pretty good job explaining its options. Select "Install", choose your keyboard layout, then type in your hostname; pcname.localhost.home or something resembling that manner. Then choose the optional system components as you wish, the default options are fine. Finally, when it comes to the "Disk Paritioning", choose "Shell".

Paritioning

gpart show

gpart destroy -F ada0

gpart create -s gpt ada0
